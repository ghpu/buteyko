Buteyko Breath Holding Exercise
==

Setting up :
- Close your mouth
- put your hands on your lower ribcage

Exercice :
- Breathe in slowly and lightly through the nose
- Breathe out slowly
- Hold your breath, pinch your nose
- Nod or sway your head for as long as you can
- Breathe back through your nose for 30s - 1 min 
-Repeat 6 times.
